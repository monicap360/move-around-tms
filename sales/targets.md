# Revenue Risk Audit: Target List
**First 25 Companies**

---

## Target Criteria

**Must Have:**
- 50+ trucks
- Ticket-based billing
- Industry: Construction / Aggregates / Waste / Hauling
- Regional operators (not national mega-fleets)

**Preferred:**
- Multiple sites/operations
- Growth-focused leadership
- History of disputes or compliance concerns
- 6+ months of historical data

## Contact Research Tasks

**For each target:**

1. **Identify company** - Name, location, size
2. **Find decision maker** - CFO / Operations VP / VP of Finance
3. **Research contact method:**
   - Email format (try: firstname.lastname@company.com)
   - LinkedIn profile
   - Company website contact
4. **Log in tracker** - Status, notes, next steps

---

## Outreach Priority

**Tier 1 (First 10):**
- Best fit (size, industry, growth stage)
- Contact info confirmed
- High probability of interest

**Tier 2 (Next 10):**
- Good fit
- Contact info partial
- Medium probability

**Tier 3 (Remaining 5):**
- Potential fit
- Contact research needed
- Lower priority

---

## Research Resources

- LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Company websites
- Industry directories
- Trade associations
- Public records (DOT filings)

---

## Status Tracking

**Status options:**
- Research - Gathering contact info
- Contacted - Email sent
- Responded - Positive/negative/neutral
- Call Scheduled - Date/time set
- Call Completed - Outcome noted
- Proposal Sent - Awaiting decision
- Engaged - Audit in progress
- Closed - Won/lost

---

## Notes Template

**For each company, note:**
- Key decision maker
- Company size/structure
- Industry specifics
- Potential pain points
- Referral sources
- Outreach attempts
- Response/outcome

---

## Success Metrics

**Track:**
- Companies researched
- Contacts identified
- Emails sent
- Calls scheduled
- Calls completed
- Proposals sent
- Engagements won

**Goal:** First 25 targets → 10 conversations → 2-3 engagements

---

**Remember: Focus on quality over quantity. Better to have 25 well-researched targets than 100 cold contacts.**
