### **SECTION 5: Sales Process**

**Step 1: Outreach**
- Email 10 executives (executive-outreach-email.md)
- Subject: "Quick question about ticket disputes"
- 5-7 sentences, no attachments
- CTA: 15-minute call

**Step 2: Discovery Call (15 min)**
- Understand operations
- Identify key pain points
- Qualify fit
- Schedule follow-up if interested

**Step 3: Proposal**
- Use revenue-risk-offer.md template
- Customize pricing based on size/complexity
- Deliver within 48 hours

**Step 4: Engagement**
- Secure read-only data access
- 30-60 day analysis window
- Weekly check-ins
- Executive presentation at end
