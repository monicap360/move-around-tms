# Niche Focus - US–Mexico Manufacturing 3PLs

## Target Customer
Small-to-mid 3PLs moving manufacturing and industrial freight between the US and Mexico.

## Why This Niche Wins
- Cross-border compliance pain is real and recurring.
- Local tools are weak; US systems are too heavy and expensive.
- We can win with speed, automation, and mobile-first execution.

## Killer Feature
**Cross-border automation**: CFDI 4.0 + Carta Porte 3.0 readiness, carrier validation, and audit packet workflow.

## Supporting Moats
- Best-in-class mobile experience (upload → exceptions → audit packet).
- Executive ROI visibility (time saved, disputes avoided).
- Partner enablement for Mexico implementation.
