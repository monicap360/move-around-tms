## Primary Sales Sentence

**"We prove your weights, show where you're losing money, and make audits painless."**

## Usage

- Add to app header (preferably visible on all pages)
- Use on landing page
- Include in sales materials
- Use as tagline in marketing

## Variations

1. **Short version:** "Prove weights. Find lost revenue. Win audits."
2. **Detailed version:** "We prove your weights are accurate, show exactly where you're losing money, and make audits painless with automated evidence packets."
3. **Aggregates-specific:** "We prove your scale weights, show where you're losing revenue, and make audits painless for aggregates operators."

## Key Messages

- **Prove weights:** Confidence scoring, baseline comparison
- **Show lost revenue:** Revenue at risk dashboard, exception tracking
- **Win audits:** Evidence packets, dispute defense

## Design Notes

- Keep it simple and direct
- Focus on outcomes, not features
- Use clear, action-oriented language
- No jargon, no "AI magic"
- Speak to executive pain points
